  • 02/12/1998 - SUCS sells RedHat Linux 5.2 on two CDs (i386 and source) at £3.50 per CD
  • ??/12/1998 - galaxy.sucs.swan.ac.uk upgraded from RedHat 5.1 to 5.2
  • 26/01/1999 - great-atuin.sucs.swan.ac.uk upgraded to RedHat 5.2

  • 05/02/1999 - Nominations open for 1999/2000 committee elections
  • 08/02/1999 - SUCS Room (3rd Floor, SU Building) opens to members
  • 19/02/1999 - djelibebi.sucs.swan.ac.uk donated by jezz

  • 25/02/1999 - SUCS AGM
  • 01/03/1999 - djelibebi.sucs.swan.ac.uk kernel upgrade to 2.2.2

  • ??/??/1999 - FireFury's photos of the SUCS room
  • ??/??/1999 - SUCS hosts live coverage of the Micromouse competition held at Swansea Uni
