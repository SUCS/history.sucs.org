Hardware

The society had three machines, hosted in room 404 on the 4th floor of Electrical Engineering (Faraday Tower). This was The Speech Lab, and the door used voice recognition. To unlock it, you had to say "Seafish" three times into a microphone in the door-frame. The SUCS machines were:

sucs.sucs.swan.ac.uk

  • Processor: 486 66MHz -> AMD K6 166MHz
  • RAM: 32MB -> 64MB
  • Storage: 1960MB IDE HDD
  • Operating System: Red Hat Linux 4.2
  • Services: WWW, FTP, Telnet, Mail, X11, Samba

galaxy.sucs.swan.ac.uk

  • Processor: 40MHz 386
  • RAM: 8MB
  • Storage: 300MB IDE HDD, 87MB SCSI HDD
  • Operating System: Red Hat Linux 4.1
  • Services: FTP, Telnet, Milliways

sunrise.sucs.swan.ac.uk

  • Processor: Sun IPC
  • RAM: 16MB
  • Storage: 200MB SCSI HDD
